Hobbies

The first hobby I’ve ever picked up was ballet. I started going to classes when I was around 4 years old, but not long after I quit (reason is unknown). As of now, I wish my parents forced me to continue, but it’s too late for that.

The second hobby I remember having was swimming. I had swimming lessons for a couple of years and I really enjoyed it. It was especially fun because I was able to go swimming with my friends and to the beach without my parents worrying about my safety (drowning). Then, I stopped going to lessons (again, the reason is unknown).

During the time I still had swimming lessons I started playing around with gymnastics with my friends. I think that started from watching youtube videos and then we decided we wanted to try it out. Unlike the first two hobbies, I didn’t go to classes and it was just self taught (somehow I never got hurt). I haven’t really lost this hobby, but I have a lot less time to do it and it happened more when I was with my friends.

The fourth hobby I’ve picked up is reading. I’ve always been a fast reader so I get through books fast, and for some reason it makes it more enjoyable. I guess it’s partially because I’m impatient and finishing a book gives me a satisfied feeling. I also enjoy reading because I’m able to picture everything in my head and how each character is portrayed, unlike movies where basically everything about a character is given to you and you aren’t able to see the character how you would like them to be.

Another hobby I’ve picked up is drawing. I’ve been going to classes for a couple of years now and I still plan on continuing. Though I’m not a great artist, it does help with projects that include art and is also a good way of spending time when you’re bored.

The most recent hobby I’ve picked up is baking. Since there was the lockdown because of covid, I haven’t been able to go out and buy my weekly dose of sweets. So the way I decided to cope with that is by making them myself. Not only do they taste good, they’re also healthier, and gives me a sense of accomplishment when I make something and it comes out good.
